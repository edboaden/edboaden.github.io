Ed Boaden
Product Design

I am a product designer, working across UX, UI and branding. I am passionate about building elegant systems – simplifying complex processes to create intuitive and engaging user-centred design.

I currently work at Ochre – a music-focussed e-commerce platform started by Bleep, Warp Records’ online store – developing the feature set to build websites and stores for an ever-increasing array of exciting artists and brands.
